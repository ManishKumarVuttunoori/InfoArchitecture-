Openwords is a Social Enterprise

Openwords is a "triple-bottom-line" for-profit. This means Openwords has social goals in addition to financial goals. Openwords app will be open source and free.

Openwords socials goals are:

  • Free educational material.
  • Open educational material.
  • Diverse educational material for diverse communities, offering flexible learning.


Mobile App

The Openwords design has different learning modules. We have single word problems like hearing, review, self-evaluate recognition, type-evaluation. We also have multiword and simple sentence translation problems in development, and a handwriting learning module for Chinese in development (utilizing the Wikimedia Stroke Order project!). The Openwords design is extensible. As we (or the community) develop new learning modules or games, we can add them in.

The Openwords app has a minimalist design. We believe languages themselves are beautiful. We also want to bring the Kickstarter community into the design process as we refine our current design. We're happy to share more about our design concepts; we've thought deeply about these issues, but the process is open to you.

Importantly the learner (and teacher) will be free to follow a curricula or express autonomy, starting in the simple ability to choose the words they want to learn.
